About Ixopo Fresh Farms

Our Story

Ixopo Fresh Farms is an agricultural business based in Ixopo, KwaZulu-Natal, focused on producing and supplying high-quality fresh produce to retailers, wholesalers and bulk buyers.

Our goal is to connect farms directly with the market, ensuring that buyers receive fresh produce while farmers get fair opportunities to sell their crops.

What We Do

Crop Production

We grow crops such as butternut, cabbage and other seasonal vegetables using fertile soil and careful farming practices.

Bulk Supply

We supply produce in bulk quantities to wholesalers, retailers and food distributors.

Market Connection

We aim to build a platform where farmers and buyers can connect directly for trade.

Our Vision

Our vision is to build a trusted agricultural brand that supports local farmers while supplying fresh produce to markets across South Africa.
